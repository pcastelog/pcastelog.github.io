Child Safety Standards

Our application is a tool for searching and discovering bars and hospitality venues. It does not allow users to create public profiles, send messages to other users, upload user-generated content, or engage in any form of social interaction within the app.

We have zero tolerance for any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, behavior, or activity that exploits, abuses, or endangers minors is strictly prohibited.

As the app does not include social networking, dating features, private messaging, or user-to-user communication, there is no mechanism for direct interaction between minors within the platform.

If we become aware of any misuse of the application for illegal purposes, we will take appropriate action, which may include reporting to the relevant authorities where required.
